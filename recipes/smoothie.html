Smoothie

Description

A smoothie using frozen mixed berries, bananas that were bought fresh, peeled, halved, and frozen, unsweetened oat milk, and a little bit of orange juice.

Ingredients

  • Frozen mixed berries
  • Frozen banana halves
  • Oat Milk
  • Orange Juice

Steps

  1. In a blender, add berries to taste
  2. Add two frozen banana halves
  3. Add oat milk to submerge fruit
  4. Add two splashes of orange juice
  5. Blend until smooth
